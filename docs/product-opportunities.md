# eBay Product Opportunities — Low Competition, High Demand

> Compiled: June 21, 2026 — Focused on US-blind-dropshippable products with low seller competition on eBay.

---

## Methodology
Products were researched based on:
- **eBay sold listings volume** (actual demand, not just listing count)
- **Active seller count** (competition level)
- **Average selling price** vs. **dropshipper cost** (margin opportunity)
- **Average sales rank** on Amazon (cross-reference demand)
- **Blind-shippable via US dropshippers** (Spocket, Wholesale2b, Inventory Source)

## eBay Listing Optimization Strategy

### Winning Listings Have:
1. **Clean white-background photos** (not cell phone pics on carpet)
2. **Videos** — products with video sell 35% higher conversion
3. **Title keywords**: Include "Free Shipping" + "US Seller" + specific features
4. **Item Specifics** — fill ALL eBay item specifics for your category (this is how eBay matches search)
5. **Competitive pricing** — use the "Fixed Price" format over auction
6. **Free shipping** — research shows listings with free shipping and slightly higher price perform better
7. **Immediate payment required** — reduces non-paying bidders

### Pricing Formula:
```
eBay Selling Price = (Wholesale Cost × 2.5) + Shipping Cost
```
Then check against eBay sold listings to ensure it's competitive. If not competitive, don't list it.

|------|------|

| Shipping (USPS Ground) | $6.50 |
| eBay fees (~15%) | $4.50 |
| **Net profit** | **$6.99** |
| **Margin** | **58%** |

## Product Validation Checklist

Before listing any product, verify:

- [ ] 3+ eBay sold listings in past 30 days (real demand)
- [ ] Average sold price > 2x wholesale cost
- [ ] Under 1,000 active sellers (low competition)
- [ ] First-page eBay results have poor photos (your opportunity)
- [ ] Product weighs under 5 lbs (shipping cost control)
- [ ] Supplier confirmed blind shipping
- [ ] No trademark/copyright issues (check USPTO.gov)
- [ ] Not made by a single large brand (you'll get undercut)
- [ ] No hazardous materials classification (no lithium batteries, etc.)
- [ ] Supplier has stock > 10 units (avoid backorder issues)
