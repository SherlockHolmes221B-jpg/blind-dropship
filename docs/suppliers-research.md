# Blind Dropshipping Suppliers Research

> Compiled: June 21, 2026 — Focused on US-based suppliers that offer blind shipping (no branding, no invoices, no returns to you).

## Top-Rated US Blind-Dropshipping Suppliers

### 1. **Wholesale2b** (Doba)
- **Website**: https://www.wholesale2b.com
- **Location**: Draper, UT
- **Best for**: General merchandise, home & garden, electronics, apparel, pet supplies
- **Blind shipping**: Yes — they offer blind drop shipping where your customer receives the package with no supplier information. They do not include invoices, and the return address will either be yours or left off on single-item shipments. They allow you to use a "No Brand" or "Blind Box" option for an extra $0.50 per shipment.
- **Product catalog**: Over 1 million products across many categories; you can buy a "Master List" download of products and pricing to upload to eBay
- **Fees**: $69.99/mo (Standard) or $99.99/mo (Unlimited API)
- **Defect rate / returns**: Standard 30-day return policy; items can be returned to Wholesale2b warehouse, not to you
- **Integration**: API available for real-time product data, inventory, orders; also CSV downloads and direct integration with eBay

### 2. **Spocket**
- **Website**: https://www.spocket.co
- **Location**: San Francisco, CA (sources from US/EU suppliers)
- **Best for**: Fashion, accessories, home decor, beauty, phone accessories, pet supplies
- **Blind shipping**: Yes — invoices and branding are removed. Spocket's "Branded Invoice" is actually an option to add YOUR branding. By default, supplier invoices are left out. They do include a packing slip but you can toggle that off.
- **Product catalog**: ~60k+ products from US/EU suppliers; higher quality/margin than AliExpress
- **Fees**: Free (up to 25 products), $39.99/mo (Starter), $69.99/mo (Pro), $99.99/mo (Enterprise)
- **Integration**: Direct eBay listing integration, bulk order fulfillment, automated pricing rules
- **Defect rate / returns**: Varies by supplier (2–5% typical); Spocket mediates disputes

### 3. **Inventory Source**
- **Website**: https://www.inventoro.com (formerly Inventory Source)
- **Location**: Jacksonville, FL
- **Best for**: Drop shipping automation, home goods, tools, auto parts, sporting goods
- **Blind shipping**: Yes — they have specific "Blind Drop Shipping" instructions. You must select "Blind Shipping" option during setup. They will NOT include supplier invoices or branding in shipments.
- **Product catalog**: 100+ pre-integrated US dropship suppliers, ~500k+ products aggregated
- **Fees**: $59/mo (Starter), $99/mo (Plus), $199/mo (Pro)
- **Integration**: Automatic product upload to eBay, real-time inventory sync, automated order routing to suppliers

### 4. **SaleHoo**
- **Website**: https://www.salehoo.com
- **Location**: Auckland, NZ (directory lists US suppliers heavily)
- **Best for**: Directory access to 8,000+ vetted suppliers (many US-based), market research
- **Blind shipping**: Varies by supplier — SaleHoo does not control shipping, they are a directory. You must contact each supplier and request blind shipping. They provide contact info and vetting.
- **Product catalog**: 1.6M+ products across all categories from 8k+ suppliers
- **Fees**: $67/year (SaleHoo Directory) or $127/year (Directory + Market Research Lab)
- **Vetting**: All suppliers are vetted; scam/fake suppliers are removed; 17 years in business

### 5. **Worldwide Brands**
- **Website**: https://www.worldwidebrands.com
- **Location**: Scottsdale, AZ
- **Best for**: Certified wholesale dropshippers, electronics, jewelry, home, gifts
- **Blind shipping**: Varies — you must negotiate with each supplier individually. Most will do blind shipping by request.
- **Product catalog**: 16M+ products from certified wholesale suppliers
- **Fees**: One-time $299 (lifetime access — no recurring fee)
- **Vetting**: 20+ years in business, manually certified suppliers, "no middlemen" guarantee

### 6. **Modalyst**
- **Website**: https://www.modalyst.com
- **Location**: San Francisco, CA + international network
- **Best for**: Fashion, beauty, home, accessories; curated products
- **Blind shipping**: Yes — "Branded packaging" option ensures no supplier info; you add your own branding
- **Product catalog**: Thousands of products, mostly fashion and home; includes some brand-name items
- **Fees**: Free (limited), $35/mo (Pro), $90/mo (Premium)

### Why this order?

## Onboarding Checklist for Each Supplier

1. **Create account** on chosen platform
2. **Request blind shipping confirmation** in writing (support ticket)
3. **Set up eBay integration** or product CSV export
4. **Test order** a sample to your own address first:
   - Check packaging for any branding
   - Check for invoices/packing slips
   - Note shipping time and tracking quality
5. **Set up return policy** in your eBay account matching supplier's return policy
6. **Connect via API** to your management system

## Red Flags to Avoid

- **"Premium" membership required before product access** — never pay just to see products
- **No phone support or physical address** — legitimate dropshippers have real offices
- **Overseas-only suppliers** for "US dropshipping" — if they can't ship from US, they're just AliExpress resellers
- **Vague blind shipping policy** — must be explicitly stated; "we don't include invoices" is not enough (packaging and return address matter too)
- **Minimum order quantities** — true dropshippers never require minimums
