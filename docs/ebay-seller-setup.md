# eBay Seller Account Setup Guide

> Create an eBay seller account to list products and start selling.

---

## Prerequisites

- **PayPal Business account** (or managed payments setup)
- **Bank account** (for eBay managed payments / payouts)
- **Phone number** (for verification)
- **Credit/debit card** (for seller fees)
- **Valid email address**

---

## Step-by-Step Setup

### 1. Create an eBay Account
- Go to https://www.ebay.com
- Click **"register"** (top left)
- Choose **"Create a personal account"** (you can upgrade later)
- Fill in: Name, Email, Phone, Password
- Verify email via the link eBay sends

### 2. Upgrade to a Seller Account
- Go to https://www.ebay.com/sellerhub
- Click **"Start Selling"** or **"Create a seller account"**
- Provide:
  - **Credit/debit card** (for insertion fees and listing fees)
  - **Phone verification** (SMS code)
- You do NOT need to start a Store right away — sell as a basic seller first

### 3. Set Up eBay Managed Payments
eBay now requires **Managed Payments** (no more PayPal direct on eBay.com):

1. In **Seller Hub** → **Payments**
2. Click **"Set up payments"**
3. eBay will ask for:
   - **Bank account** (routing + account number) for payouts
   - **Your SSN or EIN** (identity verification)
   - **Business name** and address
4. eBay will verify this information (1–2 business days typically)

⚠️ **Important**: eBay Managed Payments is mandatory in 2026. You cannot accept PayPal directly on eBay anymore. eBay collects payments and deposits to your bank account.

### 4. Choose How to Sell
- **Basic Seller** — Free to start, pay per listing ($0.35 insertion fee per listing, then ~13.25% final value fee)
- **eBay Store** — $24.95/mo (Basic Store) → 250 free listings/month + lower fees

**Recommendation**: Start as basic seller. After 50+ sales and consistent revenue, upgrade to an eBay Basic Store for the fee savings.

### 5. Set Up Your Seller Profile
- Go to **Account** → **Site Preferences** → **Selling Preferences**
- Configure:
  - **Return policy**: Set to "30-day returns, buyer pays return shipping" (matches most dropshippers)
  - **Shipping**: Set handling time to "2 business days"
  - **Payment**: Must accept eBay Managed Payments
  - **Location**: United States

## Post-Setup Checklist

- [ ] **Verify identity** — upload driver's license or passport if eBay requests it
- [ ] **Verify bank account** — confirm micro-deposit amounts (2 small deposits, ~$0.01–$0.99)
- [ ] **Set payment preferences** → Immediate payment required for Buy It Now
- [ ] **Set shipping preferences** → Free shipping on eligible items (improves search ranking)
- [ ] **Set return preferences** → 30-day return policy
- [ ] **Set business policies** → Create shipping, payment, and return policy templates
- [ ] **Add a profile photo** and **store description**
- [ ] **Read eBay's Prohibited Items policy** — know what you can't sell

| Limit | Starting Value | Increases After |
|-------|---------------|-----------------|

| Total sales value | $5,000–$10,000 | 90 days of selling |

## Monthly Costs to Expect

| Cost | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Insertion fee (per listing) | $0.35 first 250, $0.35 each after |
| Final value fee | 13.25% of total sale (average) |
| Optional Store subscription | $24.95/mo |
| Promoted listings (optional) | 2–10% additional fee |
| International fees (if selling cross-border) | 1.65% additional |

**Example: Sell a $30 item:**
- Insertion fee: $0.35
- Final value fee: ~$4.00 (13.25%)
- Gross after fees: $25.65
- Supplier cost (assume $10): $15.65 gross profit
- Shipping: $5 (if not free)
- **Net profit**: ~$10–$11

## Important eBay Policies for Dropshipping

eBay allows dropshipping with these rules:

1. **You must source from a wholesale supplier** — NOT from another retailer (Amazon, Walmart, etc.)
2. **You are responsible for shipping** — even if a supplier messes up, the customer complains to YOU
3. **You must handle returns** — customers return to you, not the supplier
4. **No drop shipping from AliExpress** — eBay cracks down on this (long shipping times = bad customer experience)
5. **Items must ship within handling time** — usually 1–2 business days

> ✅ **Using Wholesale2b, Spocket, or Inventory Source = compliant** (they are legitimate wholesalers/dropshippers)

## Building Feedback (Critical for First Sales)

| Strategy | How |
|----------|-----|
| Buy a few cheap items | Buy 5–10 cheap items as a buyer, get seller to leave feedback for you |
| Price slightly below market | First 20 sales, reduce margin to get sales volume and reviews |
| Offer free shipping | Listings with free shipping sell more despite higher price |
| Excellent customer service | Reply to messages within 1 hour, resolve issues immediately |
| Follow up for feedback | After delivery, message buyer: "Hope you love it — if so, please leave feedback!" |

**Target**: 50+ positive feedback (99%+) to be seen as a credible seller.
